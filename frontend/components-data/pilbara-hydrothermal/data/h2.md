**Hypothesis:** the chlorite assemblage formed during regional burial metamorphism (greenschist facies) long after deposition, with no involvement of high-T seafloor fluids.

**Key predictions:**
- δ18O of chlorite within +10 to +14 ‰ (lower-T, sediment-derived fluid signal).
- Chlorite cross-cutting primary igneous textures and overprinting earlier mineralogy.
- Regional consistency in alteration intensity, not localized to permeable horizons.

**Status:** plausible alternative. Cannot be ruled out without the EMPA isotope data; some textural evidence (chlorite overprinting plagioclase laths in unit B-7) is consistent with H2 in places.
