'''Standard PCR'''

This page describes the lab's standard PCR setup.

== Materials ==
* '''Plasmid template''' (10 ng/µL)
* Primers, forward and reverse (10 µM each)
* REDExtract PCR Mix (Sigma)

== Equipment ==
* Thermocycler (Bio-Rad C1000)
* PCR strip tubes

== Procedure ==
# Place PCR tubes on ice.
# Mix per reaction: 21 µL water, 3 µL primers, 6 µL plasmid, 30 µL REDExtract.
# Aliquot 19.8 µL into two extra tubes for technical replicates.
# Run on the thermocycler with the [[Standard PCR cycle]] profile.
# Run 2 µL on a 1% agarose gel to confirm the band.

See also [[Cloning workflows]] for downstream steps.
