**Primary question:** what is the dominant mechanism of enzyme inactivation as pH drops from 7 to 5?

**Working framing:** the candidate mechanisms are
1. Disruption of an active-site His-residue hydrogen-bond network (H1).
2. Cofactor displacement from its binding pocket (H2).
3. Reversible aggregation (H3) — currently considered ruled out on the strength of Smith 2024.

The question is *which*, not *whether* — we already see the activity drop. The whole project is set up to discriminate H1 from H2.
