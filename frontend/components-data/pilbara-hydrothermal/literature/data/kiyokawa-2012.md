Kiyokawa et al. 2012 document a hydrothermal alteration sequence in the Pilbara that closely matches what we see in the Marble Bar window: chlorite-rich zones interpreted as proximal seafloor hydrothermal alteration, with mass-balance signatures consistent with high-T fluid-rock interaction.
